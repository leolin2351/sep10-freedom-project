business

A business is defined as an organization or activity engaged in commercial, industrial, or professional activities with the intention of making a profit. It can take various forms, including companies, partnerships, and sole proprietorships, and involves the production, buying, and selling of goods and services. The primary purpose of a business is to generate economic value through these activities.

the history behind businesses

The original entrepreneurs were, of course, traders and merchants. The first known instance of humans trading comes from New Guinea around 17,000 BCE, where locals exchanged obsidian, a black volcanic glass used to make hunting arrowheads for other needed goods.
