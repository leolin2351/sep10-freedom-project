Context

Business is the process of applying contextual information to enhance consumer experiences and decision-making. It means knowing the specifics of a business interaction, such as the user's preferences, time, and location. With data analytics and machine learning, businesses can quickly modify their products, services, and marketing strategies to suit the individual demands of every customer. This tactic not only increases involvement but also cultivates customer satisfaction and loyalty. In the end, context business aims to improve the relationships between businesses and their customers by providing them with a more relevant and customized experience.
